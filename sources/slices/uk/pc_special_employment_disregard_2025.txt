The State Pension Credit Regulations 2002 (UKSI 2002/1792)

Schedule VI

2. In a case of earnings from employment to which sub-paragraph (2) applies, £20.

(2) This paragraph applies to employment—

(c) in the manning or launching of a lifeboat if the employment is part-time;
(d) a member of any territorial or reserve force prescribed in Part I of Schedule 6 to the Social Security (Contributions) Regulations 2001.
